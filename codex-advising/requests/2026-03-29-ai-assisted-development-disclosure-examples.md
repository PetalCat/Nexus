# Codex Research Request

## Topic

AI-assisted development disclosure examples

## Question

What good examples exist of open-source projects disclosing AI-assisted development in README, CONTRIBUTING, or docs, and what disclosure patterns seem most credible and balanced?

## Why It Matters

Nexus was heavily built with AI assistance and may benefit from a transparent but measured disclosure pattern.

## Relevant Project Context

- README work
- `CONTRIBUTING.md`
- `codex-advising/findings/2026-03-29-ai-development-disclosure.md`

## Desired Research Type

- examples
- policy/disclosure patterns
- source-backed guidance

## Desired Output

- example collection
- pattern summary
- synthesis for Claude

## Output Requirements

- include direct examples with links
- note whether each example lives in README, CONTRIBUTING, docs, or policy text
- separate examples from recommendations
- avoid turning the output into a final policy mandate
